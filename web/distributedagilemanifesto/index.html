What is the Distributed Agile Manifesto?

It is supposed to be kinda like the agile manifesto, which we find very cool. However, we want to focus on the fundamentals for successfully developing software in distributed agile teams.

Ok... where is the manifesto?

Sorry, there is nothing yet. This initiative has just been announced (today is October 18th, 2013). We are still gathering people to work on it. The first version of the manifesto, however, is expected to be released by November.

Awesome! Can I help?

Yeah! We are looking for collaborators who can share their experiences and help us shape the future of software development. Please subscribe to distributed-agile@googlegroups.com, introduce yourself and let's do it together!
